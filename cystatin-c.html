Are any contraindications present?
Edit
These conditions confound cystatin C. If present, results may be unreliable.
Acute kidney injury
Not validated for rapid changes in kidney function
Uncontrolled thyroid disease
Hyper → falsely ↑ CysC; Hypo → falsely ↓ CysC
High-dose corticosteroids
Dose-dependent ↑ CysC production
Active systemic inflammation / elevated CRP
Cytokine-mediated ↑ CysC production
Pregnancy
Not validated; physiological changes confound interpretation

Use eGFRcr-cys when GFR accuracy is critical for dosing:
DOACs
Apixaban, rivaroxaban, dabigatran, edoxaban — underdose → thrombosis; overdose → bleeding
Metformin
Borderline eGFRcr 30–45: confirm with eGFRcr-cys before discontinuing or continuing
Vancomycin
Elimination better predicted by CysC equations, especially elderly or low muscle mass
Digoxin
Narrow TI, renally cleared — use eGFRcr-cys when Cr unreliable
Gabapentin / Pregabalin
Renally cleared — use eGFRcr-cys in borderline ranges
Nephrotoxic agents
Aminoglycosides, cisplatin, high-dose MTX — accurate GFR critical
⚠ BSA adjustment: At extremes of body size, de-index eGFR = eGFR × (patient BSA ÷ 1.73). Use the calculator's BSA option.

Always use eGFRcr-cys (CKD-EPI 2021 combined equation) — most accurate for most patients.

eGFRcr 45–59 pathway: If eGFRcr-cys or eGFRcys >60 → may NOT have CKD, reclassify. If ≤60 → CKD confirmed, stage with eGFRcr-cys.

Discordance (>15–20%): Use combined equation — never rely on single marker. Investigate confounders. eGFRcys << eGFRcr = independent CV risk factor.

If combined equation still discordant with clinical picture → consider measured GFR (iohexol clearance) or timed urine CrCl.

• eGFRcr-cys ≥60 + no albuminuria → CKD unlikely. Routine monitoring not required.

• eGFRcr-cys <60 → Stage per KDIGO. G3a/A1 = annual monitoring.

• CKD G3+: annual Hgb. CKD G3a–5: periodic Ca, PO₄, vitamin D, PTH.

Refer to Nephrology: eGFR <30, UACR >300, rapid decline (>5 mL/min/yr), unexplained hematuria, refractory HTN, suspected glomerular disease.
